Exit attribution to setups:
FIFO (First In, First Out): exits close out your earliest entry first. Partial exits are credited entirely to the first tranche until fully closed, then move to the next. Mirrors how most brokers match closing fills against open lots.
Pro-Rata (by size): every exit is split across all open setups proportionally by size. If ORB is 40% of your position and the add is 60%, every partial is divided 40/60 between them regardless of fill order.

How should exits be attributed back to each setup?
FIFO (First In, First Out): exits close out your earliest entry first. If your ORB entry filled before your breakout add, partial exits are credited entirely to the ORB shares until they're fully closed, then the next exits move to the add. This mirrors how most brokers actually match closing fills against open lots.
Pro-Rata (by size): every exit is split across all open setups proportionally to how much size each contributed. If ORB is 40% of your total position and the add is 60%, every partial sale is divided 40/60 between them regardless of which was entered first. This spreads outcome evenly rather than favoring whichever setup happened to be filled first.
